ATP Professional Tennis Statistics Visualization

This visualization represents all player matchups between male tennis players at the professional level, organized by year. This project was built to highlight record-breaking tennis performances over the course of a single tournament season by an individual player. The featured years

This tool allows tennis fans to explore the matchups and compare specific player's win percentage over the course of a single tournament season.


Data provided by Jeff Sackmann and the Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP).
Visualization created by Kyle Moon-Wright.
